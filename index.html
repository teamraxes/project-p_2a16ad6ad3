Three generations · one block

Forty-six years on
40th Street.

Daniel started the shop in 1980, behind the same loading dock his grandkids park behind today. We've watched the East Bay rebuild itself three times. We're still here, and we still know which Kohler trim was discontinued in 1998.

— The Moran family Daniel · Eric · Jenna
1980

Daniel opens the doors.
A storefront, a back room, and a Yellow-Pages listing. First account: a contractor up on Piedmont Avenue who's still calling.

1992

Kohler Showroom built out.
The faucet wall goes in. We start specifying for the first wave of Oakland Hills rebuilds after the fire.

2004

Eric joins, second generation.
The truck doubles. Grohe and Hansgrohe come online. Saturdays go from a maybe to a yes.

2018

Bathroom Showroom upstairs.
We add the second floor — tubs, showers, the Grohe wall — and a real lighting plan.

2026

Third generation, same block.
Jenna is on the counter most Tuesdays. Forty-six years in, we still answer the phone in two rings.
